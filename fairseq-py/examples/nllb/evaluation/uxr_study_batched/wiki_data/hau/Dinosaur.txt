Wasu ne herbivorous, wasu carnivorous, ciki har da albarkatun-ciyar daji, daji-ciyar daji, insectivores, kuma omnivores. Duk da yake dinosaurs sun kasance na haihuwa bipedal (kamar da dukan zamani birds), wasu prehistoric nau'i ne quadrupeds, da kuma wasu, kamar Anchisaurus da Iguanodon, iya tafi kamar yadda sauƙi a kan biyu ko hudu kwai. Cranial canje-canje kamar horns da crests ne na kowa dinosaur siffofin, da kuma wasu mutuwa species da jiniya armor. Ko da yake da aka sani ga manyan size, da yawa Mesozoic dinosaurs kasancewa mutum-sized ko karami, da kuma zamani birds ne yawanci kananan a size. Dinosaurs yau zaune a kowace nahiyar, da kuma fossils nuna cewa sun cimma duniya rarraba da akalla da Early Jurassic kakar. Modern birds zaune mafi samuwa wurare, daga ƙasa zuwa teku, kuma akwai bincike da cewa wasu ba-avian dinosaurs (kamar Microraptor) iya tashi ko a kalla slide, da kuma wasu, kamar spinosaurids, da semiaquatic abũbuwan amfãni.
