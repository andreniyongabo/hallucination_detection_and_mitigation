Muhimmin mutane tafiya ya faru a cikin Pacific a prehistoric lokuta. Mutane na zamani na farko isa yammacin Pacific a Paleolithic, a kusa da 60,000 zuwa 70,000 shekaru da suka wuce. Tare da asali daga farkon mutanen fitarwa daga Afrika, sun isa Philippines, New Guinea, da kuma Australia ta hanyar tashin hankali tashin hankali na Sundaland da Sahul. Wadannan tunani daga wadannan farkon migrations a yau ne sosai daban-daban Australo-Melanesian mutane (ciki har da Negritos, Papuans, da kuma Native Australians). Wadannan kungiyoyi ba su da jirgin teku ko sailing fasaha da kuma haka za su iya fuskantar kawai short gizo na ruwa. Their mazauna a maritime kudu maso gabashin Asiya, coastal New Guinea, da kuma Island Melanesia daga baya intermarried tare da zuwa Austronesian settlers, sakamakon a zamani mutane na Island kudu maso gabashin Asiya da kuma Oceania.
