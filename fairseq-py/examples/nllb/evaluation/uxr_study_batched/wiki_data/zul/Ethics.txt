I-ethics ye-virtue ichaza umlingani womuntu oyi-moral njenge-driving force for ethical behavior, futhi isetshenziselwa ukuchaza imfundiso yama-philosophers amakhulu aseGreece njenge-Socrates no-Aristotle, futhi ama-philosophers amakhulu ase-Indian njenge-Valluvar. U-Socrates (469-399 BC) wayengomunye wamafilosofi abokuqala baseGreece okukhuthaza kokubili abafundi nabantu abadala ukuba bahamba ingqalelo yabo kwihlabathi angaphandle kwimeko yabantu. Kulesi sizathu, ulwazi okwenza impilo womuntu kwagcinwa phezulu, kuyilapho zonke ezinye ulwazi kwaba secondary. Ukuqonda ngokwami kubhekwa njengesidingo sokuphumelela futhi ngokwemvelo yimpahla ebalulekile. I-self-aware person izosebenza ngokugcwele ngaphakathi kwamakhono wayo kuya ekupheleni kwakhe, kuyilapho umntu ongaziwayo uzokungcebeleka nezinkinga. Ku-Socrates, umuntu kufanele akwazi zonke izenzakalo (futhi imibiko yayo) ezibalulekile ekusebenzeni kwakhe, uma ufuna ukufinyelela nokuqonda ngokwayo. Wabheka ukuthi abantu ngokwemvelo uya kwenza lokho okuhle uma bazi ukuthi yini ilungile. Imisebenzi embi noma embi kukhona imiphumela yokungazi. Uma i-criminal wayezazi ngempela imiphumela ye-intellectual ne-spiritual yemisebenzi yayo, yena noma yena angayenza noma ucabangele ukwenza lezi zinyathelo. Noma ngubani uyazi ukuthi yini efanelekayo ngokwenene kuzokwenza ngokuzenzakalelayo, ngokuvumelana neSocrates. Nakuba wahlanganisa ulwazi nge-virtue, wahlanganisa ngokufanayo ne-virtue ne-joy. Umuntu oyinyaniso ngempela uyazi ukuthi yini ilungile, ukwenza lokho okuhle, futhi ngakho-ke uyajabula.
