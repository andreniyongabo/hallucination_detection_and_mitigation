Ukuvakasha okubaluleke kakhulu kwamadolobha kwenzeke eNtabeni YasePacific ngezikhathi ezindala. I-human modern first reached the western Pacific in the Paleolithic, cishe eminyakeni angu-60,000 kuya ku-70,000. Ukusuka ekuqaleni kwezokuhamba kwezilwandle ezivela e-Afrika, baye bafika e-Philippines, eNew Guinea, nase-Australia ngokusebenzisa izindonga zaseSundaland neSahul. I-descendants of these early migrations today are the highly diverse Australo-Melanesian peoples (kuhlanganise neNegritos, amaPapuans, nama-Native Australians). Lezi zinhlangano azinalo ibhodi noma ubuchwepheshe wokuhambisa futhi ngakho-ke angakwazi ukuhambisa kuphela imizuzu emfushane. Izilwane zabo ezindaweni zasolwandle zase-South-East Asia, eNew Guinea, naseMelanesia Island kamva bahlanganyele nabavela abahlali be-Austronesian, okuholela ekusungulweni kwamadolobha wamanje zase-South-East Asia nase-Oceania.
