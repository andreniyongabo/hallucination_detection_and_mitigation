Line dancing is practiced futhi bafunde ezweni-ntshonalanga dance amabha, amakilabhu zomphakathi, amakilabhu dance futhi ballrooms. It is sometimes combined on dance programs with other forms of country-western dance, njenge-two-step, i-western promenade dances, kanye ne-western style variants of the waltz, polka and swing. I-line dances yahlanganyela ngezitayela eziningi zomculo odumile kusukela ekuqaleni kwe-1970s kuhlanganise ne-pop, swing, rock and roll, disco, Latin (salsa suelta), rhythm and blues ne-jazz.
