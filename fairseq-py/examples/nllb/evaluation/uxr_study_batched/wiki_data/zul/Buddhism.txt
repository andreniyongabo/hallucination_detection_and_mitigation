I-truth ye-dukkha iyimfihlo eyisisekelo ukuthi ukuphila kulo lonke ihlabathi, ne-sticking ne-craving kuya kwezimo nezinto ezingapheli, yi-dukkha, futhi engapheleleki. I-Dukkha inguqulwe ngokuthi "ukukwazi ukuhlangabezana," "umuntu ongenakupheli kanye nokungabazeki jikelele kuzo zonke izenzakalo ezivamile"; noma "ukuphazamiseka." I-Dukkha is ngokuvamile iguqulwe ngokuthi "ukubulawa," kodwa lokhu kungalungile, ngoba isho ngeke sikhumbule ukuphazamiseka okuphindaphindiwe, kodwa ngokwemvelo okungafanelekanga kwezimo nezinto, kufaka phakathi nakho okuzithakazelisayo kodwa okuzisheshayo. Sithemba injabulo kusukela izimo nezinto ezingapheli, futhi ngakho-ke ayikwazanga ukufeza injabulo yangempela.
