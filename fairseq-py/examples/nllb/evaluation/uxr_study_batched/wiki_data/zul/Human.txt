I-Genes ne-environment isebenza ekushintsheni kwe-biological yabantu ngezimpawu ezibonakalayo, i-physiology, ukuzwela kwesifo, izakhono zobuciko, ubukhulu bomzimba kanye nesikhathi sokuphila. Nakuba abantu abahluka ngezimpawu eziningi (njenge-genetic predispositions and physical features), amadoda amabili ngokwesilinganiso angaphezu kuka-99% efanayo, nabantu abahlukahlukene kakhulu ngokwemvelo abavela e-Afrika. Isilinganiso esiphezulu se-genetic iyahlukahluka phakathi kwamadoda nabesifazane. Ngokwesilinganiso, amadoda anazo amandla amaningi emzimbeni, futhi abesifazane ngokuvamile banezinga eliphezulu kwesisindo somzimba. Izingane zidlula i-menopause futhi zibe neziminyaka ngaphambi ekupheleni kweminyaka yabo. Futhi babe ukuphila isikhathi eside cishe wonke umphakathi emhlabeni wonke. Ukuhlukaniswa kwamadoda namadoda ama-gender roles iye yahlukahluka ngokwedlulele, futhi izibophezelo ezivela ezindala zesini ziye ezivela ezindala ezindaweni eziningi.
