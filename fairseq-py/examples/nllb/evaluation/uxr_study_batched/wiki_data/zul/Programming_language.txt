I-computer programming language iyilimi elisetshenziselwa ukubhala izinhlelo zekhompyutha, okubandakanya ikhompyutha ekusebenziseni uhlobo lwezibalo noma i-algorithm futhi kungenzeka ukulawula amadivaysi angaphandle afana ne-printers, ama-drives, ama-robots, nokunye. Ngokwesibonelo, izinhlelo ze-PostScript ngokuvamile zenzelwe uhlelo olunye lokulawula iphrinta noma ukubonisa kwikhompyutha. Ngokuvamile, i-programming language ingaba incazelo yokubala kwabanye, mhlawumbe abstract, umshini. Kuyabonakala ngokuvamile ukuthi Ukucaciswa Okugcwele kwelwimi lokuprograma kuhlanganisa incazelo, kungenzeka ukuthi kufanelekile, kwimishini noma processor kulelo ulimi. Ngezinye izinhlangothi ezivamile, ulimi lokuprograma ihlanganisa ikhompyutha; Ngakho-ke, izilimi zokuprograma zihlolwe futhi zihlolwe ngale ndlela. Izilimi zokufunda zihlukana nezilimi zemvelo lapho izilimi zemvelo zisetshenziselwa ukuxhumana phakathi kwabantu, kuyilimi lokufunda futhi ivumela abantu ukwazisa imiyalelo kumadivayisi.
