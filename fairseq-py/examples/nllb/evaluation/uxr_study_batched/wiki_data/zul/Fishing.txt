Ukudoba kuyindlela endala okuvela okungenani ekuqaleni kwenkathi ye-Upper Paleolithic cishe iminyaka engu-40,000 edlule. Ukuhlaziywa kwe-isotopic yabantu be-Tianyuan, abantu abangu-40,000 ubudala abadala base-East Asia, kubonisa ukuthi bahlanza njalo imfishi yamanzi omnandi. Izici zokuvakasha ezinjenge-shell middens, ama-fish bones, nama-cave paintings zibonisa ukuthi ukudla kwamanzi kwakubaluleka ngokulondolozwa nokulondolozwa kwezinga elikhulu. Ukudoba e-Afrika kubonakala kuqala kakhulu emlandweni womuntu. I-Neanderthals ibalwa cishe i-200,000 BC. Ngase abantu baye bakwazi ukuthuthukiswa isikhwama for fish traps, futhi spinning futhi izindlela ekuqaleni zokuthunga ukuze wenze nethuba lokuloba ukuze bakwazi ukubamba okuningi izilwane ezinkulu.
