Ngenxa yokuba ngamanzi, ama-vitamins angaphezu kwe-B ngokuvamile aphume kalula, nakuba ukusetshenziswa, ukusetshenziswa kanye ne-metabolism ngabanye kungahluka. Iziguli ezindala kanye nezemidlalo kungase kudingeka ukwengeza umthamo wabo B12 nezinye ama-vitamins B ngenxa yezinkinga ekuchitheni kanye nezidingo zokukhiqizwa kwamandla. Ngezinye izimo ezinzima kakhulu, ama-vitamins B, ikakhulukazi i-B12, angafakwa nge-injection ukuze ziphendukele ukwehluleka. I-Type 1 ne-Type 2 ama-diabeticians angase adluliselwe ukwengeza i-tiamine ngokusekelwe ku-high prevalence of low plasma thiamine concentration and increased thiamine clearance associated with diabetes. Futhi, i-Vitamin B9 (i-folic acid) iphutha ekuthuthukiseni kwe-embryo ekuseni ihlanganiswe ne-neural tube defects. Ngakho-ke, abesifazane abafuna ukukhulelwa ngokuvamile kunconywa ukuba ukwandisa ukudla kwansuku zonke folic acid umthamo futhi / noma uthathe isizinda.
