Mesopotamia literally yana nufin "a cikin rairayin bakin teku" a cikin gargajiya Yaren mutanen Girka. Mafi tsohon sanin halaye na sunan Mesopotamia date to 4th karni BC, a lokacin da aka yi amfani da su yi magana da ƙasar gabas da Euphrates a arewacin Syria. Sa'an nan, shi ne mafi general amfani ga dukan ƙasar tsakanin Euphrates da kuma Tigris, da haka hada da ba kawai sassa na Syria amma kuma kusan duk Iraq da kuma kudu maso gabashin Turkey. The jirgin steppes zuwa yamma da Euphrates da kuma yammacin Zagros Mountains ne kuma sau da yawa hada da a karkashin mafi girma sharuddan Mesopotamia. Wani ƙarin bambance-bambance ne kullum sanya tsakanin Upper ko Northern Mesopotamia da kuma kasa ko Southern Mesopotamia.
