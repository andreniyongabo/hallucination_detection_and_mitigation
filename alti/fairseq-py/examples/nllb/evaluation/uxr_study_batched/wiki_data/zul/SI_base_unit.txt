Imibuzo mayelana nemikhiqizo yethu noma pricelist, sicela ushiye ikheli lakho kithi thina sizothintana emahoreni angu-24. Ngokwesibonelo, i-meter (US English: meter) ine-symbol m, kodwa i-kelvin ine-symbol K, ngoba isibizwa ngokuthi nguLord Kelvin futhi i-ampere ne-symbol A isibizwa ngokuthi ngu-André-Marie Ampère.
