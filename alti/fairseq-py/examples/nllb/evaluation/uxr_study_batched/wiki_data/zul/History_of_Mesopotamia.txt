I-Mesopotamia literally means "amaphakathi nezimfoloko" ngesiGreek lasendulo. Umlando owodwa owaziwa igama Mesopotamia ivela ku- 4th century BC, lapho isetshenziselwa ukuchaza izwe ngasempumalanga ye-Euphrates enyakatho yeSyria. Ngemva kwalokho yayisebenza ngokubanzi kunazo zonke izwe phakathi kwe-Euphrates ne-Tigris, ngaleyo ndlela ebandakanya ingxenye yamaSyria kuphela kodwa futhi cishe yonke i-Iraq ne-South-East Turkey. I-steppe eliseduze ngasempumalanga ye-Euphrates ne-Western part of the Zagros Mountains nayo ivame kufakwe ngaphansi kwegama elikhulu le-Mesopotamia. Ukuhlukaniswa okwengeziwe ngokuvamile kwenziwa phakathi kwe-Upper noma i-Northern Mesopotamia ne-Lower noma i-Southern Mesopotamia.
