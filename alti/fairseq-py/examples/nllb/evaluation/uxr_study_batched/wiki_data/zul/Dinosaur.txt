Ngo-2016, inani eliphakanyisiwe le-dinosaur yezinhlobo ezikhona e-Mesozoic yaba ngu-1,543-2,468. Ngonyaka ka-2021, inani lamadoda zanamuhla (ama-avian dinosaurs) kubalwa ku-10,806 izinhlobo. Ngezinye izinhlobo zihlanganisa ama-herbivorous, ezinye zihlanganisa ama-carnivorous, kufaka phakathi ama-seed-eaters, amafish-eaters, ama-insectivores, nama-omnivores. Nakuba ama-dinosaurs wangaphambilini be-bipedal (njengoba zonke izindiza zanamuhla), ezinye izilwane zangaphambilini zingenama-quadrupeds, nabanye, njenge-Anchisaurus ne-Iguanodon, bakwazi ukuhamba kalula ngezinyawo ezimbili noma ezine. Izinguquko ze-cranial ezifana ne-horns ne-crests ziyi-common dinosaur traits, futhi ezinye izinhlobo ezikhishwe zine-bone armor. Nakuba yaziwa ngobukhulu obukhulu, ama-dinosaurs amaningi we-Mesozoic wayesayizi womuntu noma amancane, futhi izilwane zakudala ngokuvamile zincane. Dinosaurs namuhla zihlala zonke izifundazwe, futhi izitshalo zibonisa ukuthi baye sifinyelele ukusatshalaliswa global okungenani ekuqaleni Jurassic era.
